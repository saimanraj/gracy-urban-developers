Urban Local Body (ULB) Building Approval

At Gracy Urban Developers, we provide complete Building Approval services under Urban Local Bodies (ULB), strictly following all Government rules and planning regulations.

We handle approval processes in:

  • ✔ Corporation
  • ✔ Municipal Council
  • ✔ Town Panchayat

When ULB Approval Is Required?

1. Residential Buildings

If the total built-up area is below 10,000 sq.ft, approval must be obtained from the Urban Local Body (Corporation / Municipality / Town Panchayat).

2. Commercial Buildings

If the commercial building area is below 2,000 sq.ft, building approval must be taken from the respective Urban Local Body authority.

We ensure the approval process is completed legally, correctly, and without delays.

Documents Required for ULB Building Approval

To process the approval smoothly, the following documents are required:

  • Mother Document
  • Patta
  • Land Approval Document
  • Chitta & Adangal / A-Register Extract
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC) – Minimum 10–20 years
  • FMB Sketch
  • Field Measurement Map (Re-Survey Number)
  • NA Conversion Document (if required)
