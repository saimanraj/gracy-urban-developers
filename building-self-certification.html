Self-Certification Building Approval (ULB – Single Window Portal)

At Gracy Urban Developers, we assist in obtaining Self-Certification Building Approval through the Urban Local Body (ULB) Single Window Portal, strictly following Tamil Nadu Government regulations.

Self-Certification is a simplified and fast-track approval system for small residential buildings that meet specific conditions.

When Self-Certification Approval Applies?

Self-Certification can be used for Residential Buildings that satisfy both criteria below:

1. Built-up Area Condition

The total built-up area must be below 3,500 sq.ft.

2. Plot Size Condition

The land/plot area must be below 2,500 sq.ft.

If both conditions are satisfied, approval is issued by the Urban Local Body (Corporation / Municipal Council / Town Panchayat) through Self-Certification Mode.

We handle the entire submission, verification, drawing preparation, and approval follow-up.

Documents Required for Self-Certification Approval

To process the approval smoothly, the following documents are required:

  • Mother Document
  • Patta
  • Land Approval Document
  • Chitta & Adangal / A-Register Extract
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC) – Minimum 10–20 years
  • FMB Sketch
  • Field Measurement Map (Re-Survey Number)
  • NA Conversion Certificate (if agricultural land converted to non-agricultural use)
