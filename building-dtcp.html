DTCP & Urban Local Body Building Approval

At Gracy Urban Developers, we provide Building Approval services strictly according to the Tamil Nadu Government rules and regulations. Our team handles complete approval procedures in DTCP (Directorate of Town and Country Planning) and Urban Local Bodies such as Corporation, Municipality, and Town Panchayat.

When DTCP Approval Is Required

According to government norms, DTCP approval is mandatory in the following cases:

1. Residential Buildings

If the total built-up area exceeds 10,000 sq.ft, DTCP approval is required.

2. Commercial Buildings

If the commercial building exceeds 2,000 sq.ft, DTCP approval is compulsory.

3. Special Category Buildings

DTCP approval is required for:

  • Hospitals
  • Schools & Colleges
  • Hostels & Marriage Halls
  • Industries & Godowns
  • Large Commercial Complexes
  • Any building that exceeds threshold area under planning rules

4. Industrial Buildings

DTCP approval is needed for:

  • Factory Buildings
  • Godowns
  • Industrial sheds above 1,000 sq.ft
  • Hazardous category industries

Fire Safety Approval (NOC Requirement)

If the building height exceeds 18.3 meters, a Fire Safety NOC from the Fire & Rescue Services Department is mandatory.

Documents Required for DTCP Building Approval

To process the approval smoothly, the following documents are commonly required:

  • Mother Document
  • Patta
  • Land Approval Document
  • Chitta & Adangal / A-Register Extract
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC) – Minimum 10–20 years
  • FMB Sketch
  • Field Measurement Map (Re-survey Number)
  • NA Conversion (if agricultural land converted to non-agricultural use)
  • ULB (Panchayat) NOC, wherever applicable

(Depending on the case, additional documents may be requested by DTCP/ULB.)
