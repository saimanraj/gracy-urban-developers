Regularisation of Unapproved Plots
(DTCP – Corporation, Municipality, Town Panchayat)

We, GRACY URBAN DEVELOPERS, specialize in obtaining Regularisation Approvals for Unapproved Plots by ensuring complete compliance with Government rules and regulations.

We assist in securing approvals from:

  • ✔ DTCP (Directorate of Town & Country Planning)
  • ✔ Corporation
  • ✔ Municipal Council
  • ✔ Town Panchayat

Every plot must be regularised and approved under the norms of DTCP or the respective Urban Local Body as per Government Order (G.O) No. 78.

We provide complete documentation support, submission, and follow-up until approval is obtained.
