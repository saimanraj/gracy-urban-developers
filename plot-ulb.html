Unapproved Plot Approval – ULB

GRACY URBAN DEVELOPERS is a trusted consultant for obtaining approvals for unapproved plots, ensuring full alignment with Tamil Nadu Government rules and planning standards. We provide professional guidance for clearing plot irregularities and obtaining formal approval from Urban Local Bodies (ULB) such as:

  • ✔ Corporation
  • ✔ Municipal Council
  • ✔ Town Panchayat

According to G.O. No. 78, every unapproved plot must be regularised under the norms of either DTCP or the relevant ULB before it can be sold, developed, or used for construction. Failure to regularise may lead to legal restrictions, penalties, or rejection of building applications in the future.

Our role is to make the process simple and stress-free. We assist with:

  • Document collection & verification
  • Application drafting & submission
  • Layout / survey alignment
  • Continuous follow-up with ULB officers
  • Final approval handover

With our experience, we ensure your plot becomes fully legal and compliant—without delays or complications.
